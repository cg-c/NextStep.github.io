Data Science

Data science is the field of study that combines domain expertise, programming skills, and knowledge. A data scientist's job is to analyze data for actionable insights. Specific tasks include: Identifying the data-analytics problems that offer the greatest opportunities to the organization. Determining the correct data sets and variables.
