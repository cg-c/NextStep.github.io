Our Mission

We believe that a lot of students may feel lost when it comes to deciding what they want to do specifically with their career in technology before and after college. This project aims to provide a resource to students and those considering going into the tech field, to help find an industry they feel passionate about. Providing resources for students in the computer science field is needed.

What We Offer

Our goal is to create a website that will become a resource for women and non-binary students interested in the Computer Science field but who don't know what their NextStep is. We hope the website will introduce new and niche fields of technology like human-computer interaction, user experience, health informatics, information and coding theory, as well as research opportunities to help expose these opportunities to people that they may not have heard of before. We know this will be successful because it's made by students for students.
